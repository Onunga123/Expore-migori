Explore Migori County: Discover Kenya's Hidden Gem

Welcome to Migori County, a treasure trove of natural wonders, cultural heritage, and captivating history nestled in the heart of southwestern Kenya. Here's a glimpse of what awaits you:

  1. Thimlich Ohinga World heritage Site: Step back in time at the legendary Thimlich Ohinga, a 14th-century stone-built complex that stands as a testament to ancient architectural ingenuity. Explore the massive stone walls and immerse yourself in the rich history of early settlements in the Lake Victoria basin.

  2. Gogo Falls: Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of Gogo Falls, a hydroelectric power station surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Walk across the narrow steel bridge and feel the rush of the River Kuja as it cascades into Lake Victoria. Don't miss the chance to cast your line for sport fishing in the dam's tranquil waters.

  3. Mugabo caves: Journey to the shores of Lake Victoria and uncover the secrets of Mugabo Caves, once a hideout during colonial wars. Marvel at the stunning views from atop the caves and feel a sense of peace as you explore this sacred site, steeped in local tradition and spirituality.

  4. Chinato Crying Stone: Discover the cultural significance of Chinato Crying Stone, a historic hill in Kuria East where locals sought refuge during colonial conflicts. Hike to the top for panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and immerse yourself in the timeless stories of resilience and community.

  5. Sori and Senye beaches: Relax and unwind on the black sandy shores of Sori Beach or bask in the sun on the white sandy expanse of Senye Beach. Indulge in fishing expeditions, boat rides, and leisure activities as you soak in the tranquil beauty of Lake Victoria.

  6. Aluru and Migingo Islands: Embark on an adventure to Migingo Island, a bustling fishing hub with a vibrant nightlife and stunning rocky landscapes. Explore the waters around Aluru Island, where fishing competitions and camping expeditions await amidst the serene beauty of Lake Victoria.

  7. Accommodation:Experience comfort and convenience at our selection of hotels and resorts, including Florence Hotel, Hotel Discretion, Hill View Hotel, and Sugar Land Hotel. Whether you're seeking budget-friendly options or luxurious retreats, Migori County has accommodations to suit every traveler's needs.

  8. Nightlife:: Dance the night away in Migori Town, where a burgeoning entertainment scene offers a mix of bars, clubs, and live music venues. Join locals and visitors alike as they unwind and celebrate life in the heart of Migori County.

    9. Come and explore Migori, where adventure, culture, and relaxation await at every turn. Start planning your journey today and discover the magic of Kenya's hidden gem.
